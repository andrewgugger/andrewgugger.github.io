ANDREW GUGGER

Hello :)

Thanks for visiting my About page. I was born in Switzerland but grew up in the Northeast where I started rowing in highschool and it became my passion. The valuable lessons I have learned from rowing have helped me to succeed in my endeavours both in and outside of the classroom.


Rowing has given me a work ethic and a multitude of life lessons that has helped me in every discipline I have pursued. It taught me the true power of hard work, dedication, and patience. I struggled a lot during my first semester at university; I was unsure of what to major in, I hadn't performed as well as I would have liked in rowing, and I was missing many of my friends from home. I decided that I needed a win. Having only ever ran approximately 8 miles before, I impuslively signed up to run the Philadelphia marathon with only 4 weeks to train. I ran everyday while simultaneously attending 4:30 AM crew practice and balancing a busy course load. I worked my way up to running 20 miles the weekend before the race and I ended up completing it in 3:42. This accomplishment reinforced in me the importance of goal setting and dedication and how rewarding it can be.

After taking the spring semester of my freshmen year off I traveled to my home country of Switzerland where I had the amazing opportunity to stay with my family where I could work on a construction site and race at regattas. I met some amazing people on both the construction sites and my rowing team, Ruder Club Schaffhausen. My coach, Ueli, was one of the most inspirational and the wisest human being I have ever met and getting coached by him was a truly humbling experience. I am truly grateful and honored to have called him my coach and I only could have met him through rowing. His elite coaching reinvigorated my passion for rowing and I ended up returning the following summer to once again race with them. From working on construction sites to racing at many high-profile regattas such as the Swiss Championships, I was also able to connect with my country on a much greater level.


Aside from rowing and school I also enjoy motorcycling and am an amateur botanist!

Here are some of my latest projects...


On March 1, 2020, a new bag waste reduction law took effect in New York State - where over 23 billion plastic bags are used each year. Plastic bag usage affects both our communities and environment. They can be seen stuck in trees, as litter in our neighborhoods, and floating in our waterways. My brothers and I started New York Relic in the wake of the ban with the mission to provide reusable carry bags inspired by the iconic designs found on the plastic bags of New York. On top of pursuing my degree and rowing, we have been working around the clock fulfilling orders, creating designs, and utilizing social media for guerilla marketing.
